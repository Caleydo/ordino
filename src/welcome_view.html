Welcome to Ordino

Ordino is a browser-based visual data analysis solution to flexibly rank, filter, and explore
genes, cell lines, and tissue samples based on a rich set of experimental and metadata.

Ordino contains data from The Cancer Genome Atlas (TCGA), the Cancer Cell Line Encyclopedia (CCLE),
as well as two depletion screen data sets from McDonald et al. (Cell 2017) and Meyers et al. (Nat. Genet. 2017).
